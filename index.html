All about Greece

Greece is famous for its ancient philosophers, like Plato, Pythagoras, Socrates, and Aristotle, to name a few. It is known as the birthplace of democracy in the West; they invented the Olympic Games and theater.

    What its famous for
  1. Beaches
  2. Greek Olives
  3. Islands

Native animals of Greece include brown bears, wolves, lynx, deer, Martens, and wild boar. Goats and donkeys are popular domesticated animals. The waters are home to huge, gentle sea creatures, and its forests are filled with rare birds, bears, deer and lynxes
